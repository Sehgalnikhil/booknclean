Our Story

We've Seen Good Tenants Lose Their Bond Over a Missed Spot — That's Why We Built This

Bond cleaning isn't about making a house look nice.
It's about knowing exactly what an agent inspects — and never missing a single item.

Moving out is stressful enough without losing your bond over something you didn't even know an agent checks. We've seen it happen too many times — tenants who cleaned for days, hired someone off Gumtree, handed back a tidy property, and still got hit with a deduction.

The problem isn't that they didn't clean. It's that their cleaner didn't know what a real estate agent actually looks for. There's a difference between clean and inspection-ready — and that gap costs tenants real money.

So we built Book & Clean around one rule: clean to the real estate agent's standard, every single time — or come back and fix it for free. We now serve renters across Perth, Melbourne, Brisbane and Sydney, with more than 800 bond cleans behind us. Still family-run, not a franchise.

Location Coverage

Bond Cleaning in Your Metro Area — How It Works

Your final inspection isn't about whether the place "looks clean" — your property manager walks room by room comparing it against the Property Condition Report you signed when you moved in. The items agents flag most often are the oven, shower screens, window tracks, skirting boards and wall marks. Our checklist is built around exactly those items, and every quote comes with the full scope of work in writing.

We cover suburbs across Perth, Melbourne, Brisbane and Sydney metro areas and surrounds. Same fixed price by property size wherever you are, with morning and afternoon slots most days.

Got carpets? Most agencies ask for a professional steam-cleaning receipt at handover — add carpet steam in the quote form and the receipt is included for your agent. And if your property manager flags any cleaning item within 72 hours of your inspection, we come back and re-clean it free, typically within 2–3 business days.

What we doExterior glass on the ground floor, external frames and sills wiped, fly screens removed and washed where they come out without tools. Inside windows on every floor are already included free.Not includedUpper-storey exterior glass (safety — that's a specialist job), screen or seal repairs.

FAQ

Common questions answered

Everything you need to know before booking your bond clean.

What exactly is included in an End of Lease Clean?+
Our End of Lease Cleaning includes detailed cleaning of all living areas, bedrooms, bathrooms, laundry, kitchen, interior windows, doors, skirting boards, built-in cupboards, oven, cooktop, rangehood, and carpet steam cleaning. Garage and balcony floors are also swept.
Is oven deep cleaning included?+
Yes. Professional oven, cooktop, rangehood, and filter cleaning are included as part of our End of Lease Cleaning service at no extra cost.
Is wall cleaning included?+
Wall spot cleaning is included as standard. Full wall washing is an optional add-on for heavily marked walls.
How does the Bond Back Guarantee work?+
We provide a 72-hour Bond Back Guarantee. If your property manager or real estate agent reports any cleaning-related issues within 72 hours of your exit inspection, we'll return and rectify those items at no additional cost.
Do I need to pay a deposit?+
No deposit is required. Payment is due after the clean is complete and you are satisfied.
Do you clean external or upper-floor windows?+
External ground-floor window cleaning is available as an optional add-on. Upper-floor exterior glass is a specialist job for safety reasons.
Does the property need to be vacant?+
Yes. For the best results and to meet our Bond Back Guarantee requirements, the property must be completely vacant before cleaning begins.
What do I need to provide before the clean?+
Please ensure electricity, running hot water, and suitable parking for our service vehicle are available throughout the cleaning service.
Is carpet steam cleaning included?+
Carpet steam cleaning is an option you select during the quote process — it's included in the "With Carpet" package at a flat rate.
Can I add extra services after booking?+
Yes. Additional services can usually be added before your appointment, subject to availability. Contact us as early as possible.
Do you service multiple cities?+
Yes. We provide professional bond cleaning services across Perth, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Sydney.
What payment methods do you accept?+
Payment is required upon completion. We accept major credit/debit cards through our secure payment link.
