Our Story

We've Seen Good Tenants Lose Their Bond Over a Missed Spot — That's Why We Built This

Bond cleaning isn't about making a house look nice.
It's about knowing exactly what an agent inspects — and never missing a single item.

Moving out is stressful enough without losing your bond over something you didn't even know an agent checks. We've seen it happen too many times — tenants who cleaned for days, hired someone off Gumtree, handed back a tidy property, and still got hit with a deduction.

The problem isn't that they didn't clean. It's that their cleaner didn't know what a real estate agent actually looks for. There's a difference between clean and inspection-ready — and that gap costs tenants real money.

So we built Book & Clean around one rule: clean to the real estate agent's standard, every single time — or come back and fix it for free. We now serve renters across Perth, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Sydney, with more than 3000 bond cleans behind us. Still family-run, not a franchise.

Location Coverage

Bond Cleaning in Your Metro Area — How It Works

Your final inspection isn't about whether the place "looks clean" — your property manager walks room by room comparing it against the Property Condition Report you signed when you moved in. The items agents flag most often are the oven, shower screens, window tracks, skirting boards and wall marks. Our checklist is built around exactly those items, and every quote comes with the full scope of work in writing.

We cover suburbs across Perth, Melbourne, Brisbane, and Sydney metro areas and surrounds. Same fixed price by property size wherever you are, with morning and afternoon slots most days.

Got carpets? Most agencies ask for a professional steam-cleaning receipt at handover — add carpet steam in the quote form and the receipt is included for your agent. And if your property manager flags any cleaning item within 72 hours of your inspection, we come back and re-clean it free, typically within 2–3 business days.

Add-ons — exactly what you get

Most cleaning companies sell add-ons as a name and a price. Here's precisely what each one covers, and what it doesn't.

Carpet steam cleaning +
What we doPre-vacuum, hot-water extraction (the method agents ask for), spot pre-treatment of stains, high-traffic area treatment, deodorise. You get a receipt for your agent.Not includedPermanent or pre-existing stains, burns, bleach marks, pet odour soaked into the underlay, carpet repairs or re-stretching.
Outside windows — ground floor +
What we doExterior glass on the ground floor, external frames and sills wiped, fly screens removed and washed where they come out without tools. Inside windows on every floor are already included free.Not includedUpper-storey exterior glass (safety — that's a specialist job), screen or seal repairs.
Blind cleaning — chemical +
What we doSlat-by-slat chemical wash of venetians and verticals, plus cords, rails and fittings. Standard dusting and wiping of blinds is already included free — this is for built-up grime.Not includedBrittle or sun-damaged blinds (we'll flag these first), curtain or fabric drape laundering.
Full wall wash +
What we doEvery wall surface washed top to bottom with sugar soap, including doors, frames and skirting. Wall spot cleaning (light marks and scuffs) is already included free — this is for heavily marked walls.Not includedPaint damage, flaking, holes, or stains absorbed into the paint — those need a painter.
Garage clean +
What we doCobwebs removed, floor swept and mopped, garage door and frame wiped inside, fixed shelving wiped down.Not includedOil and grease stains on concrete (needs pressure washing), rubbish or furniture removal, driveways.
Balcony / patio +
What we doFloor swept and mopped, railings and balustrades wiped, sliding glass doors and tracks cleaned both sides, cobwebs removed.Not includedPressure washing of tiles or decking, plants and planter boxes, outdoor furniture.
Extra living room · study / theatre room +
What we doThe full standard scope, identical to bedrooms and living areas — windows, skirting, wardrobes and shelving, light fittings, wall spot cleaning, floors.Not includedCarpet steam for that room (add per-room steam if you need it).

And so there are no surprises, here's what no cleaning package covers: ceilings, mould remediation, pressure washing, rubbish removal, repairs of any kind, pest control, and outdoor grounds like lawns and driveways. We clean — we don't fix. If something needs a specialist, we'll tell you before we start, not after.
